About

Learn more about Athenas Vanguard.

  • 2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The brainchild of Eldsvadadods who only wanted to make a community discord and had no idea it would ever grow to such heights.

  • April 2018

    An Idea is Born

    It quickly became clear that coordinating ships together would allow them to be stronger than their individual parts.

  • Augest 2018

    Alliances Added To Game

    With the Cursed Sails update alliances became a integrated feature into the game. This mechanic allowed for greater coordination and multiplication of gold and reputation gains!

  • 2020

    Affiliate Alliances

    Rare adds Affiliate Alliances and Athena's Vanguard is one of them! With this new coordination with Rare we put on a tournament of our very own!

Flying Ships

Just when you thought you had seen it all.

Flying ships are something of a legend in Sea of Thieves. Try to explain it away the best you can: lag, bad driving, magic powers; the fact remains that the clouds will always mock us simple pirates who strive for great heights.

Unknown Waters

Are you truly lost if you know where your ship is?

Few things compare to a nice fog, sailing the unknown and uncharted seas with nothing but a compass and your trusty crew. It takes quite the coordinated crew to find islands by means other than bumping into them.

Megalodon Hunt

The poor beast doesn't stand a chance

The hunter becomes the hunted as the Megalodon is spotted and surrounded. The once fearful beast becomes trapped in a circle of boats, harpooned, and butchered for its natural resources. The King of the Megs still evades us however...

The Fleet Sets Sail

Off to conquer new seas

One section of the seas at a time the fleet sails the waters on the hunt. Megalodon, Skeleton Ship, fearsom Kraken; none of them stand a chance once the fleet is on the hunt.

Lying In Wait

The fog conceals our presence

What's worse than fleeing before a fleet on the horizon? Unknowningly sailing into the midsts of one hidden in a fog of course! The Fort of the Damned provides an excellent source of income and a perfect ambush spot for the poor fool who decides to try and sneak up on the fleet.

Trading Salvos

No no, don't help, just take pretty pictures.
